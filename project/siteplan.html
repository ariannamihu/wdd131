Site Plan

Site Name

Arianna M Photography - This will show visitors that this is a website to showcase the work and information about a photographer.

Site Purpose

As a wedding and couples photographer, my goal is to create a website that showcases my work and allows potential clients to learn more about me, my style, and my pricing. I also want to provide a way for clients to contact me easily and to highlight reviews from previous clients to build trust and credibility

Scenarios

What is the pricing of this photographer? What type of photography does she specialize in? How can I contact this photographer?

Color Schema

--baby-powder: #fcfcf8ff will be used for the background. --raisin-black: #222021ff will be used for all text.

Typography

Titles- Playfair Display, Accent words—Playfair display italics, Body—Open Sans or Lato (light 300.
